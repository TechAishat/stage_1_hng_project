Journey so far

About Aishat

I'm a web developer who transforms sketches and concepts into inclusive, multi-page experiences. Every build is a chance to merge clean design with thoughtful engineering so users feel seen, supported, and inspired.

Who I am

I'm a Web developer blending a background in product strategy with hands-on engineering. My mission is to craft digital solutions that feel intuitive, inclusive, and delightful—no matter the device or ability of the person on the other side of the screen.

Beyond the code editor, I love exploring visual storytelling, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and learning by teaching others. Curiosity keeps me iterating.

Goals in this program

  • Translate internship briefings into polished, user-friendly interfaces.
  • Strengthen collaboration with mentors and teammates through pair-programming and feedback loops.
  • Ship at least two production-ready front-end features with robust testing and documentation.

Areas of low confidence

I'm steadily growing my confidence with performance profiling and complex state management. To improve, I pair-program with peers, break problems into smaller experiments, and document the lessons I learn.

Note to future self

Remember the spark you feel when a user experiences a feature you built and smiles. Keep asking great questions, stay humble, and let empathy steer your decisions.

Extra thoughts

This journey isn't just about code—it's about community. I'm grateful for mentors, teammates, and friends who challenge me to grow, and I'm committed to doing the same for others.
